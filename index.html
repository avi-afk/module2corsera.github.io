Potato curry

Potato is known as “Aloo” in Hindi. So basically any Indian curry made with potatoes is known as “Aloo curry”. Potatoes, sautéed onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, spices and herbs come together to make this delicious curry that is absolutely perfect for weeknight dinner

Paneer

Paneer is an Indian cheese that's made from curdled milk and some sort of fruit or vegetable acid like lemon juice. There are some distinct traits that make Paneer the cheese that it is: It's an unaged cheese. It's a soft cheese ,it is very tasty to eat.A must try! Paneer is used in main dishes as well as desserts and snacks.

dosa

A dosai or dosa or dose is a thin pancake or crepe, originating from South India, made from a fermented batter predominantly consisting of lentils and rice. ... Its main ingredients are rice and black gram, ground together in a fine, smooth batter with a dash of salt, then fermented.
